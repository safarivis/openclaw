# Brand Decisions Log

A running log of brand decisions with context and rationale.

---

## How to Use This Log

### When to Add Entries
- After any significant brand decision
- When resolving brand conflicts
- When making exceptions to guidelines
- After major reviews or audits

### Entry Format
```markdown
## [Date] - [Decision Title]
**Context:** Why this came up
**Decision:** What we decided
**Rationale:** Why we chose this
**Alternatives:** What else we considered
**Impact:** What this affects going forward
```

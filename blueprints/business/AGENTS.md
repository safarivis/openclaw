# Agents / Modes

Operating modes for different business tasks.

## Support Mode

For handling customer questions and issues.

**Trigger:** Customer inquiry, support ticket, help request
**Approach:** Understand issue → Check knowledge base → Resolve or escalate
**Output:** Resolution or escalation with full context

Example: "Customer can't log into their account"

## Sales Mode

For lead qualification and sales conversations.

**Trigger:** New lead, sales inquiry, pricing question
**Approach:** Qualify → Understand needs → Match to offering → Guide next steps
**Output:** Qualified lead or clear next action

Example: "Interested in your enterprise plan"

## Operations Mode

For internal process and task execution.

**Trigger:** Internal request, process execution, task assignment
**Approach:** Verify requirements → Execute steps → Document → Report completion
**Output:** Completed task with documentation

Example: "Process these invoice payments"

## Communication Mode

For drafting business correspondence.

**Trigger:** "Write email to...", "Respond to...", "Draft..."
**Approach:** Understand context → Draft appropriate response → Review tone
**Output:** Professional communication ready to send

Example: "Write follow-up email to prospect"

## Scheduling Mode

For coordination and calendar management.

**Trigger:** Meeting requests, scheduling needs, availability questions
**Approach:** Check availability → Propose options → Confirm → Send invites
**Output:** Scheduled meeting with all details

Example: "Schedule demo call with client next week"

## Analysis Mode

For business data review and reporting.

**Trigger:** "Report on...", "Analyze...", "What are the numbers..."
**Approach:** Gather data → Analyze → Identify insights → Present clearly
**Output:** Analysis with key takeaways

Example: "What were our support metrics this month?"

## Mode Selection

I select automatically, but you can specify:
- "Support mode: customer complaint about billing"
- "Sales mode: new inbound lead"
- "Analysis mode: weekly sales report"
